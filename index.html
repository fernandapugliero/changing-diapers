FAQ: Changing Diapers in Berlin

Where can I find baby changing tables in Berlin?
You can find baby changing tables across Berlin: in shopping malls, restaurants, museums, and public restrooms. On FixMyDiaper, there are more than 160 verified locations, with new ones added regularly by parents. Whether you are near Alexanderplatz, Tempelhofer Feld, or Tiergarten, there is likely a spot nearby where you can change your baby comfortably.

Do Berlin cafés or restaurants usually have changing tables?
Some do, but it’s not universal. The good news is that Berlin is getting more family-friendly every year. On FixMyDiaper, you can type an address or use your current location, and the map will show nearby changing tables. It's perfect for emergencies or planning an outing with your baby.

How to find the nearest changing table?
Go to fixmydiaper.com, type an address or use your location, and the map will instantly show baby-friendly places around you. Each spot includes a short description, user reviews, and information on accessibility, so you can pick the best one for your needs.

Are toilets in Berlin accessible for strollers?
Many are. Facilities in malls, museums, and restaurants often include spacious family restrooms where you can fit a stroller. On FixMyDiaper, users note whether a location is stroller-friendly, so you can plan ahead.

Can I add a new location to FixMyDiaper?
Yes! FixMyDiaper is built by parents and caregivers. You can submit new locations directly on the website, adding the address, a short description, and (if possible) a photo. It takes a minute, but helps other parents all over Berlin.

Where can I find baby changing tables in Mitte?
Mitte has the largest number of changing tables listed on FixMyDiaper. Popular family-friendly spots include Humboldt Forum, Alexa Shopping Center, Dussmann Kulturkaufhaus, Manifesto Markt, L’Osteria, Zollpackhof, and Casalot Restaurant.

Are there baby changing facilities in Neukölln?
Yes! Neukölln has a growing number of family-friendly locations. You’ll find changing tables at KALLE HALLE Markthalle, K-Fetisch, La Maison, Engels, MaraLou Café, Mignon Sicilian Bakery, Nah am Wasser, and Gorilla.

Is Prenzlauer Berg baby-friendly?
Absolutely. Prenzlauer Berg has long been one of Berlin’s most family-oriented areas. FixMyDiaper lists several changing tables here, including Schönhauser Allee Arcaden, schim:Pause, Petit Brunch, Le Belford, I Due Forni, Brocca Café, and Trivitys.

Wo finde ich Wickeltische in Berlin?
In Berlin findest du Wickeltische an vielen unterschiedlichen Orten, zum Beispiel in Einkaufszentren, Cafés, Museen und öffentlichen Toiletten. Auf FixMyDiaper kannst du deine aktuelle Position oder eine Adresse eingeben und siehst sofort kinderfreundliche Orte mit Wickeltischen in deiner Nähe.
