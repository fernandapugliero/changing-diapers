Work with us

Helping families discover baby-friendly places, changing tables and activities.

Fix My Diaper is a community-driven project helping parents find places that make going out with babies and toddlers easier – from changing tables and stroller-friendly cafés to family activities and local discoveries.

Let’s work together

We help families decide where to go before they leave home.

Built by a parent, for parents

Hi, my name is Fernanda.

I created Fix My Diaper with my son when he was 3 months old.

After becoming a parent in Berlin, I realized I had to rediscover the city from scratch. Places I had known for years suddenly became new again: Can I enter with a stroller? Is there a changing table? Is it actually comfortable with a baby?

Fix My Diaper was created to make this easier for other families – through an interactive map, local guides and honest recommendations.

Why brands collaborate with us

Families do not need more ads. They need better recommendations.

Collaborations are integrated naturally into content that families actually want to save, share and use. A café, hotel, shop or family space becomes part of a useful recommendation, not a traditional advertisement.

Who we work with
