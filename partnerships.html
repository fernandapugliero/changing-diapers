Why Fix My Diaper exists

A small platform built from a very real parenting problem.

Becoming a parent completely changed how I experience Berlin.

Suddenly I started noticing the things I had never paid attention to before: changing tables, stroller accessibility, welcoming cafés and family-friendly spaces.

Fix My Diaper started as a personal project to make those places easier to find. Today, it is becoming a growing local platform helping families explore Berlin with more confidence.

Why brands collaborate with us

Families do not need more ads. They need better recommendations.

Collaborations are integrated naturally into content that helps families make decisions. A café, hotel, shop or family space becomes part of a useful recommendation, not a traditional advertisement.

Parents want practical answers: Is there space for a stroller? Is there a changing table? Is this place worth leaving the house for?
