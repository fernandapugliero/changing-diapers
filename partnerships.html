Built by a parent, for parents

After becoming a parent in Berlin, I realized I had to rediscover the city from scratch. Places I had known for years suddenly became new again: Can I enter with a stroller? Is there a changing table? Is it actually comfortable with a baby?

Fix My Diaper was created to make this easier for other families — through an interactive map, local guides and honest recommendations.

A focused local audience

Fix My Diaper reaches parents, especially mothers, who are actively looking for places to go, things to do and services that make family life in Berlin easier.

This is a highly specific audience: families with babies and young children, mostly based in Germany, interested in practical local recommendations.
