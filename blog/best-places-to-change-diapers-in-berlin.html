Best Places to Change Diapers in Berlin

Your ultimate guide to diaper-changing stations in the city

Published on: July 2025

We've explored Berlin to find the best spots for changing diapers — and we've ranked our top 5. From cafes to cultural centers, this list includes places that were tested, loved, and submitted by real parents. The ranking may change as new places are added, so check back often. And if you know a great spot, add it here!

1. MaraLou Café

A cozy neighborhood café with excellent coffee, plenty of space for strollers, a clean and accessible changing table, and warm service. MaraLou is truly baby- and parent-friendly.

2. Mignon Sicilian Bakery

Delicious Sicilian pastries, friendly staff, and a surprisingly well-equipped changing table in the restroom. A sweet spot to rest and refresh with your little one.

3. WILMA Shoppen

This shopping center in Charlottenburg features multiple changing stations, nursing rooms, and stroller-friendly paths — a practical and reliable option for families on the go.

4. Casalot Restaurant

Middle Eastern fine dining meets family-friendly atmosphere. The bathroom is clean, the changing station is well maintained, and the staff is welcoming to children.

5. Kulturhaus Insel Berlin

A unique cultural space by the river with a beer garden and a playground next to it. The facilities include a simple but clean diaper changing station — and the location is perfect for a summer outing with kids.
