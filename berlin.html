You can find changing tables across Berlin in cafés, restaurants, shopping centers, museums and public restrooms. Fix My Diaper is updated regularly by parents, so you can quickly discover baby-friendly places nearby.

Some do, but it’s not universal. The list includes community-submitted reviews, photos and notes so you can pick the best option for your needs.
