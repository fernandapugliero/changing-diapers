About Fix My Diaper

Fix My Diaper is a collaborative project focused on helping parents find baby-friendly places with changing tables. Our goal is to make it easier for moms and dads to find clean and accessible diaper changing stations in cafes, restaurants, and other public spaces. Contribute by submitting locations where you've found great diaper changing facilities to help grow this global resource for parents everywhere.
