About Changing Diapers

Our Mission

Changing Diapers is an initiative to help parents and caregivers easily find public places with diaper changing stations available for free. We strive to create a community-driven platform that can be relied upon by parents in need of clean and accessible diaper changing stations.

Join Our Community

Help us grow our database! We encourage parents and caregivers to contribute by submitting their favorite diaper-friendly spots, ensuring that we provide the most complete and accurate map possible.
