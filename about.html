About Changing Diapers

Changing Diapers is a community-driven project focused on helping parents find baby-friendly places with changing tables in Berlin and beyond. Our goal is to make it easier for parents to find clean and accessible diaper changing stations in cafes, restaurants, and other public spaces. Contribute by submitting locations where you've found great diaper changing facilities to help grow this global resource for parents everywhere.
