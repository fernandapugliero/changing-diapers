About Fix My Diaper

Fix My Diaper is a collaborative project focused on helping parents find baby-friendly places with changing tables. The goal is simple: make it easier to locate clean and accessible diaper changing stations in cafes, restaurants, shopping malls, museums and other public spaces. You can contribute by submitting locations where you've found good facilities and help grow this global resource for parents everywhere.
